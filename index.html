RULES
  1. The player places a bet before the game begins.
  2. The dealer deals two cards to the player, including themselves. One card is face-up, the other face-down.
  3. The player evaluates their hands and decides whether to "hit" (receive another card) or "stand" (keep their current hand).
  4. If the players total hand value exceeds 21, they bust and lose the round.
  5. After the player has finished their turn, the dealer reveals their face-down card.
  6. The dealer hits until they reach a total of 17 or higher.
  7. If the dealer busts, all remaining players win.
  8. If the dealer's total is higher than a player's but 21 or less, the player loses their bet.
  9. If a player's total is higher than the dealer's and 21 or less, they win and receive a payout.
  10. If both the dealer and player have the same total, it's a push, and the player gets their bet back.

    11. Remember, the objective is to have a hand total closer to 21 than the dealer without going over.
